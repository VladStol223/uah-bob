First: Open a Folder

Bob needs an open folder to do anything useful — write code, create files, remember context. Without one, it's just a chat window.

Open Folder in the Explorer panel
In the Explorer panel (left sidebar), click Open Folder. Navigate to your folder and click Select Folder. Bob is ready to go the moment the folder loads.
Use a dedicated, empty folder
Create a fresh, empty folder just for your Bob work — something like ~/bob-workspace. Bob can read every file in the open folder, so keeping it dedicated means it only ever sees what you intentionally put there. Don't open your home directory, Documents, or any folder with sensitive files.

QisBob - Quantum Computing with IBM

QisBob is a full custom Bob mode that acts as a warm, Socratic quantum mentor. It assesses your Python level, picks a learning track (Executive, Developer, or Hardware), and walks you gate by gate through Qiskit v2.0+ circuits on real IBM Quantum hardware -- quizzing you at every step.

Track B
Accelerate Your Research
Point Bob at a real research question or dataset from your lab and use it to explore, debug, or build a first working prototype.
Undergrad & Grad Researchers Sponsored projects Working prototype Your own data
UAH researchers work with NASA Marshall, NOAA, and dozens of sponsored programs. Bob can accelerate every stage -- from understanding a dataset to building the first analysis script to writing the methods section.
1
Understand a Dataset You Have Never Seen

Got handed a CSV or netCDF file with no documentation? Bob will interrogate the structure, flag anomalies, suggest what analysis approaches are appropriate, and generate the first exploration script.

Bob Prompt
Here is a dataset from my research: [describe columns, units, and what was measured]. I've never worked with this type of data before. Explain what it likely represents, suggest 3 appropriate analysis approaches, flag any data quality issues I should check for, and write a Python script that loads it and produces a summary overview.
2
Build a Working Analysis Script

Describe your research question and Bob builds the scaffold -- a Python or R script that runs, produces output, and is documented well enough to hand to your advisor or co-author.

Bob Prompt
Here is a dataset/script from my research: [describe it]. Help me understand what's in it, then build a working analysis I can run and adjust. The goal is [describe your research question]. Use pandas and matplotlib. Add comments explaining what each section does so I can learn as I go.
3
Write the Methods Section of Your Paper

Describe what you did in plain language and Bob turns it into a properly structured methods section -- with the right passive voice, standard terminology for your field, and a checklist of details reviewers will look for.

Bob Prompt
I'm writing the methods section for a research paper in [your field]. Here is what I did in plain language: [describe your methodology]. Write a formal methods section using standard academic language for this field. Then give me a checklist of details I may have left out that reviewers commonly flag in this type of study.

Track C
Solve a Real Admin or Teaching Problem
Bring a manual process, a teaching aid you wish existed, or a small tool you've been meaning to build — and use Bob to get a first working version.
Faculty & Staff No IT required Automation Real campus need
Faculty and staff at UAH spend enormous time on manual tasks that AI can automate in minutes. These prompts are for real problems -- not toy examples.
1
Automate a Weekly Manual Task

If you copy-paste, re-format, or manually compile anything on a regular schedule, Bob can build the script that does it for you. You just describe what you do and what you want out the other side.

Bob Prompt
I do [manual task] by hand every week. It involves [describe inputs, steps, and outputs]. Build me a simple Python script or automation that does this for me. Walk me through how it works so I can modify it later, and tell me what I'd need to install to run it.
2
Build a Teaching Aid or Interactive Exercise

Need a quiz, a worked example, a case study, or an interactive exercise for a course you teach? Bob can generate it from your syllabus content and format it for your LMS, handout, or slide deck.

Bob Prompt
I teach [course name] at UAH. I need a [quiz / worked example / case study / interactive exercise] on the topic of [topic]. My students are [undergrad/grad] with background in [prerequisites]. Generate the full teaching aid, including an answer key or solution walkthrough, formatted so I can paste it directly into [Canvas / a handout / a slide deck].
3
Draft a Grant or Proposal Section

Describe your project in plain language and Bob will draft the narrative sections of a grant or internal proposal -- broader impacts, project description, evaluation plan -- at a quality ready for revision, not from scratch.

Bob Prompt
I'm writing a [NSF / DOD / internal UAH] grant proposal. My project is about [describe in 3-4 sentences]. Draft the "Broader Impacts" section (300 words) and the first paragraph of the "Project Description." Then list 5 things reviewers for this funding agency typically look for that I should make sure to address.
Starting Prompt from the Bobathon Deck
"I do [manual task] by hand every week. Build me a simple tool or automation that does this for me."
